Meerkat

What are meerkats

The meerkat or suricate is a small carnivoran in the mongoose family. It is the only member of the genus Suricata.

Meerkats live in all parts of the Kalahari Desert in Botswana, in much of the Namib Desert in Namibia and southwestern Angola, and in South Africa

Meerkat's favourite activities:

  • Hunting
  • Digging burrows
  • Look for predators

Key characteristics of meerkat

  1. They weigh less than one kilogram
  2. They are omnivorous
  3. They are about 50 cm long
